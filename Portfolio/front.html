About

My name is Samantha Kobetitsch, sometimes known as Jaymie. I'm a digital artist based in Illinois, currently attending Illinois State University for a bachelor's in Creative Technology. I like drawing animals and fantastical scenes, and I'm heavily inspired by works such as the Legend of Zelda, His Dark Materials, Elder Scrolls, and various anime. I'm a cat lover that loves painting space and I'm interested in mysticism and the occult, which all will commonly find its way into my work.
